Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that is stir-fried in a wok or frying pan, typically with other ingredients like vegetables, meat, eggs, and seasonings. It's a popular dish across many Asian cuisines, known for its versatility and ability to be customized with various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice – 2 cups
  • Garlic – 4 cloves
  • Onion – 1 small
  • Carrot – 1 small
  • Capsicum – ½
  • Beans – a few
  • Spring onion – 2 tbsp
  • Soy sauce – 1 tsp
  • Vinegar – ½ tsp
  • Salt – as needed

Steps

  1. Prepare rice
  2. Heat oil
  3. Add veggies
  4. Add sauces
  5. Add rice
  6. Add spring onion
